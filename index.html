About ATCL

In the aim to provide collaborated solutions to the complex IT situations, ATCL- Aspiring Technologies Co. Ltd. has launched 12 years ago at Jeddah, Kingdom Saudi Arabia. In just a span of few years ATCL was successful in making a strong foothold in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia by the expanding its business presence with its branch locations in Riyadh and Dammam. Blending professionalism, expertise and dedication in the right proportion, ATCL strives to deliver quality solutions all the time to all our clientele which includes SMB’s and Large Enterprises.

Cheif Operating Officer

Mr. Asim is a Sr. Corporate Executive with broad-based management experience at leading international companies, leveraging a diverse 18-years background that spans management consulting, business services, and in-house corporate experience to assist corporations in implementing their most important strategic initiatives & decisions, restructurings, operations, part of business setup initial team player, IT business consulting and so on.

His philosophy with regards to business is that, it’s a four-legged chair composed of Sales & Marketing, Operations & Finance, Pre & Post-Sales Support & Logistics. One cannot survive without the other. My job is to ensure that all the legs function in full spirits and achieve their highest quality targets, as efficiently as they can, creating value & fulfilling commitments for our valued customers.

A believer in connecting diverse professionals to create a strong network that will benefit all from each other’s advice and experiences, he is constantly leveraging hsi relationships to facilitate introductions between executives who have the potential for partnership, collaboration, idea-sharing, and peer-to-peer guidance.
